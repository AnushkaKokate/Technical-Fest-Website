Game of Codes

First Price: ₹3000/-
Second Prize: ₹2000/-

Entry Fee: Free

Date: 19 & 20 April 2021


About: “Make appeal, finalize your deal, take a chance to steal the win...” Be a part of a GRAND CODING EVENT to test your INSTICTS and SKILLS.

Team Specifications: 1. The team should consists of 3-4 members.(open for all)
2. The team will be divided into two parts i.e. one member of the team will manage the dealing process and remaining members of the team will manage program execution part.

Description:
Round 1: Round 1 will be of 7-10 minutes. In this round, one member from each team will attempt a quiz. Quiz will consist of 20 questions (Non-Technical +GK).
Round 2: Round 2 will be of 1hr 30 min. In this round, each team will be provided with 2 problem statements. To implement these programs each element (for loop, if statement, switch case etc) has to be first purchased. The list of elements with their cost will be displayed initially. Each team will be given 10 minutes to discuss, decide and purchase the list of elements which they require during program implementation (list should be submitted through a provided google form). Once the team has purchased the required elements, corresponding points will be added to team’s wallet. During implementation each team will have the option to sell or purchase elements by dealing with other teams or a team can purchase an element directly from the organizers but at double cost. If any team uses an extra element which is not mentioned in their initial list then double cost of each extra element will be added to their wallet.

Rules:
1.Only one member of the team should appear for the quiz in 1st round.
2. The teams should join the meeting on time.
3. In round 1, the contestant should compulsorily submit the form or else the response will not be recorded.
4. For round 2 there will be 3 meetings, one member from each team will join the 1st meeting for dealing and remaining members will join the 2nd meeting for program implementation.
5. A 3rd meeting link will be provided where a team has to join to show their output once they have completed their implementation before time.
6. Initial list of elements should be submitted using the provided google form before starting the implementation.
7. In round 2, if any team is willing to make a deal with another team then the deal should be finalized within a minute.
8. Code file in PDF/DOC and final element list should be submitted through a provided google form within 3 minutes.
9. If you cheat, use any unfair practice or break the rules, you will be disqualified

Individual or Two members in team(Open for all students)

Campus Calling

First Price: ₹3000/-
Second Prize: ₹2000/-

Entry Fee: Free

Date: 24 April 2021


About: Campus Calling Event will be organized by MESA. In this event students will get a chance to face the pre-placement rounds which will boost their confidence before going for the campus placements.In this event, we are conducting three rounds.

Description:
Round 1: General Aptitude exam via google forms. Participating students will get a link for the exam via their mail id’s or whatsapp message. A specific time will be allotted to complete the aptitude exam and later an average score will be calculated to enter the next round.
Round 2: In the second round the students who have cleared the aptitude exam will face a group discussion with other students on a common topic provoded by the judges. Round 3: In the final round, the shortlisted students will have to face a personal interview with the judges, the judges will assess you knowledge and accordingly reward you some points. The highest point scorer, 1st place student will be rewarded a cash prize and an online certificate, and followed by the 2nd place student and the 3rd place students too. This round will be conducted on Google- Meet platform.

Rules:
1.Students participating in this event should provide an appropriate email ID and their phone number.
2.Students participating in this event must have good internet connectivity.
3.During group discussion round and personal interview round camera and microphone should be on.
4.Students should submit their CV/Resume before the personal interview round.
5.Students causing unnecessary disturbance during online group discussion round and personal interview round will be prohibited from the meeting.
