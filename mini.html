Code Relay

First Price: ₹2000/-
Second Prize:₹1000/-

Entry Fee: Free

Date: 19 April 2021


About: “You have to pass the baton, everyone takes a turn and chips in, that’s what great teams do...” Who says relay race is a physical sport...come with us to experience an unusual logical relay. CODE RELAY is not a event but a tiny effort to built team spirit around this amazing institute.

Team Specifications: The team should consists of 2-3 members.(open for all)

Description:
Stage 1: This whole stage has to be completed within 10minutes. The first teammate would get a problem statement which has to be executed and as he completes the execution and if the output is correct the 1st stage is completed.
Stage 2: This stage has to be completed within 5 minutes . In this stage the player will be provided with a virtual dice where he has to select 3 number (1-6). Once participant rolls the dice the timer will start, if the selected number appears then he has to solve 5 EASY MCQ’s(Google form will be provided) or else has to dry run the given program(Google form link). Once he successfully completes this round this stage will be completed.
Stage 3: This stage has to be completed within 8 Minutes. Once the 2nd teammate completes stage 2, 3rd teammate will be provided with a problem statement which has to be executed to complete this round.
Note : If any team completes any stage before the given time period the remaining time will be added to the next stage.

Rules:
1. Partially executed code won’t be accepted.
2. To participate in this event each and every team has to join the google meet during their respective time slot provided by the organizer.
3. For every stage the participant has to share his/her screen on the google meet.
4. If you cheat, use any unfair practice or break the rules, you will be disqualified

Scavenger Hunt

First Price: ₹2000/-
Second Prize:₹1000/-

Entry Fee: Free

Date: 23 April 2021


About: Too bored in quarantine? Get ready to sharpen your minds and put your thinking caps on!! Searching for your daily necessities is a way more frustrating than imaginable. Let's make the quarantine interesting, Join in the fun of scavenger hunt and ensure you expertise in the skills of scrutinizing. Online scavenger hunt is an event, where you will be given a series of riddles to solve followed by sets of hints. When you reach the final destination, you will be awarded.

Team Specifications: Team of 2/Solo participation

Description:
Round1: Riddles based on the Household things. Participant needs to guess and find a thing.Elimination based on the average time required to solve and find the things (No time limit).
Round2: Round 2 based on a Brain Teaser. Participant has to find the answer within a limited time period.Elimination based on the average time required to solve the brain teasers.
Round3:Technical round based on electronic gadgets and riddles. Participant have to find the answer within a limited time period.

Description:
1.Team of 2/Solo participation
2.Top 5 finalists from a round 2 will be qualified for a round 3 (final round).
3.Elimination of the participants from each round based on the average time required to solve all the riddles.
4.Cheating/misconduct by any means will lead to straight disqualification.
5.Participant must be with good internet connection, participant will be responsible of losing internet connection during the event.
